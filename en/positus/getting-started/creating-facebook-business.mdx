# Creating a Facebook Business Account

To activate your official WhatsApp number using an authorized provider, you need to create an account in Facebook Business Manager (the owner of WhatsApp) and verify your account. This way, WhatsApp ensures that your business actually exists, preventing fraud in your name or on behalf of other companies. \
This step may also be useful in the future if you use other Facebook products for your business. We suggest creating and verifying your business as soon as possible—rest assured, this procedure is free and does not require a credit card.

This process is mandatory. It is required for Facebook to confirm that you are indeed a legal representative of your company. Once verified, we will be able to set up your official WhatsApp business API account.

## Business Manager

- Access [https://business.facebook.com/](https://business.facebook.com/) and create your account for free.

![Creating an account in Facebook Business Manager](/images/positus/primeiros-passos/criar-conta-gerenciador.gif)

![Filling out the business account creation form](/images/positus/primeiros-passos/criar-conta-formulario.gif)

- If you manage more than one business, select the specific business where you want to activate the WhatsApp number.

![Selecting the desired business in Business Manager](/images/positus/primeiros-passos/selecionar-negocio.gif)

- Navigate to the upper menu, click on "Business Settings", and then click on "Security Center".

![Accessing the Security Center in Business Settings](/images/positus/primeiros-passos/centro-de-seguranca.gif)

- If the "Start Verification" button is grayed out/disabled, follow the next step; otherwise, you can skip the next section by [clicking here](/docs/positus/primeiros-passos/criando-business-facebook#verificando-meu-negócio).

![Disabled "Start Verification" button in the Security Center](/images/positus/primeiros-passos/botao-iniciar-verificacao.png)

## Enabling the Business Verification Button

Access [http://developers.facebook.com/](http://developers.facebook.com/) and create your account following the on-screen instructions.

Create an app (you can use any name for your app). After creating your app, copy the **App ID** code.

![Creating an app on Facebook Developers and copying the App ID](/images/positus/primeiros-passos/criar-aplicativo-developers.gif)

Now return to your Business Settings page, navigate to the "Apps" menu, click on "Add", and then "Connect an App ID". Paste the "App ID" code you copied in the previous step and click "Add App".

<Info>
  After adding your app, you need to refresh your page to enable the "Start
  Verification" button. Press **F5** on your keyboard to reload.
</Info>

![Connecting the App ID to the business page](/images/positus/primeiros-passos/conectar-id-aplicativo.gif)

Now go back to the verification menu: navigate to the top menu, click on "Business Settings", and then click on "Security Center".

---

## Verifying My Business

Follow the on-screen instructions. This step is extremely important and you must provide real information only; otherwise, your account will not be verified. \
You do not need to be the owner or partner of the company to complete this step, but certain documents might be requested, and you will need to ask the responsible managers for them.

![Filling out business verification details](/images/positus/primeiros-passos/verificando-negocio.gif)

Your account will likely remain under review for a few days. The Facebook team will confirm that your company exists and that the documents you submitted prove you are authorized to represent it.

![Facebook Business account under review](/images/positus/primeiros-passos/conta-em-analise.png)

<Info>
  This procedure typically takes 1 to 7 days. If it exceeds this period, we
  suggest contacting Facebook Support at
  [https://www.facebook.com/business/help/support](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/support)
  or reaching out to us.
</Info>

Once your account is verified, navigate to the "Business Info" menu, take a screenshot, and send it to Positus along with the other required information to activate your official number.

![Business Info menu after account verification](/images/positus/primeiros-passos/informacoes-da-empresa.gif)

---

## Approving the Request - Accept Positus as Your Business Partner

After sending your details to Positus, we will register your company as a business partner. At that moment, you will receive a notification on your Facebook Business page. We can only proceed with the activation of your number after you approve it. \
Go to [https://business.facebook.com/](https://business.facebook.com/) >> "Business Settings" >> "Requests" and click "Approve".

![Approving the Positus partnership request in Facebook Business Manager](/images/positus/primeiros-passos/aprovar-solicitacao-parceiro.jpeg)
