## Onboarding and Activation

1️⃣ - **Create a Positus Account** \
Sign up on our platform at [http://studio.posit.us/](http://studio.posit.us/)

2️⃣ - **Facebook Business Manager** \
Create an account at [https://business.facebook.com/](https://business.facebook.com/) and verify your business.
\
\
\*Check out our step-by-step guide on how to set it up:*\
[*Creating a Facebook Business Account\*](/docs/positus/primeiros-passos/criando-business-facebook)

3️⃣ - **Get in touch with Positus** \
Choose your desired plan, provide your Business ID and the phone number you wish to activate, then accept the partnership request from Positus inside your Facebook Business Manager console.

- **Website:** [https://posit.us/#contact](https://posit.us/#contact)
- **WhatsApp:** [https://wa.me/551126265327](https://wa.me/551126265327)

4️⃣ - **Activating your account** \
Receive the verification code via phone call or SMS from WhatsApp, and you are good to go!
